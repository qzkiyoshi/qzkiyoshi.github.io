CV, Qing Zhang

profile

Qing Zhang is a project researcher at The University of Tokyo's Rekimoto Lab, specializing in visual perception manipulation and vision engineering. In 2023, he earned his Ph.D. in Media Design from Keio University, Japan. With a decade of experience in fine arts, media arts, and graphic communication design, his research delves into the mechanisms of human vision and its information-processing patterns.

Research Keywords

Vision Augmentation, Vision Engineering, Visual Perception Manipulation, Vision Hacking, Optical See-through Augmented Reality, Video See-through Augmented Reality, Desired Reality, Ubiquitous Computing, Smart Eyewear/Glasses.
