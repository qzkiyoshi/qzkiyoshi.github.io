This is Qing Zhang

I just started work as a project researcher at Rekimoto Lab this April. My research interests are vision augmentation, computer vision interaction, visual perception modulation, smart glasses, and context-aware computing.

My PhD principle advisor is Kai Kunze, co-advisors are Kazunori Sugiura(internal), Junichi Yamaoka(internal), Thad Starner(external), and Yoichi Sato(external).


Educational Background:
Ph.D. (science&engineering), Keio University.
Master of Fine Arts, Tokyo University of The Arts.
Bachelor of Arts, Hubei Institute of Fine Arts.
